Visualization: Terminology Network in Kant's Critique of Pure Reason

Karen Lyu

Oct 14, 2021

This webpage visualizes the results from a project of mine. It denotes how important terms in Kant's Critique of Pure Reason (CPR) are related together.

The larger the nodes are, the more statistically significant they are in CPR. The thicker the edges are, the more related the pairs of nodes at the end of the edges are, that is, they are more semantically similar.

• Scroll up and down with your cursor on the image to zoom in and out.
• Drag the image to move your visual field.
• You can also drag the nodes within to rearrange the network.
